## 🚀 Project Highlights

* Built an **interactive Power BI dashboard** for tracking ecommerce sales metrics
* Designed **KPI cards** for Total Sales, Profit & Orders
* Implemented **drill-down filters and slicers** for dynamic analysis
* Created **data relationships & calculated fields using DAX**
* Applied **data modeling techniques** for structured reporting
* Designed visually rich reports using:

  * Bar Charts
  * Pie & Donut Charts
  * Clustered Bar Charts
  * Scatter Plots
  * Line & Area Charts
  * Map Visuals
  * Slicers & Filters

---

## 📊 Business Questions Answered

* What is the total revenue and profit trend?
* Which product categories drive maximum revenue?
* Which states contribute the highest sales?
* What payment modes dominate transactions?
* What are the monthly & quarterly profit patterns?
* Which customers contribute most to revenue?

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Sales and profit trends fluctuate seasonally
* Certain product categories dominate revenue contribution
* COD & digital payments form the majority share
* Top-performing states significantly impact overall revenue
* Repeat high-value customers contribute major profit share
